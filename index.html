Rachel is a multifaceted creative living in Oklahoma City.

She currently works for Teleflora helping local florists market themselves online and building their brand locally.

Rachel has a wide set of skills including video and photo production, shooting, editing and set design. She also has the tools and knowledge to help any business with their online presence through online marketing, social media and online branding. She enjoys expressing her creativity through her work whether it's managing social media pages, filming a wedding, creating graphics or collaborating with other creatives on a shoot.
